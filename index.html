About Ćejf
Ćejf (pronounced "chayf") is a Bosnian word for the slow, pleasurable enjoyment of something — particularly coffee. Built by a home barista frustrated by apps that only logged shots without explaining why they tasted wrong. Covers espresso and pour-over. Version 4. All feedback welcome.
